Awards

ICE2CPT 2024 will recognize exceptional contributions and innovations in the field of electric power and renewable energy. Below are the awards that will be presented at the conference:

Best Paper Award

This award recognizes the most outstanding research paper presented at the conference.

Prize: $1,000

Young Researcher Award

Presented to an early-career researcher who demonstrates significant potential in renewable energy studies.

Prize: $750

Innovative Solution Award

Honors a project that proposes a unique and practical solution to a critical problem in electric power systems.

Prize: $1,200

Best Presentation Award

Given to the presenter who delivers the most engaging and effective presentation during the conference.

Prize: $500

Sustainable Impact Award

Recognizes a project or research that demonstrates a long-term impact on sustainability and renewable energy.

Prize: $800
