Have questions about the NIT Jamshedpur Conference? We've got you covered! Below are the answers to some of the most commonly asked questions.

1. What is the date of the conference?

The conference will take place from 15th to 17th February 2024.

2. Where will the conference be held?

The conference will be held at NIT Jamshedpur, located at Adityapur, Jamshedpur, Jharkhand.

3. How can I register for the conference?

You can register on the official conference website by clicking the "Register" button in the menu. Early registrations are eligible for discounts!

4. Is there a registration fee?

Yes, the registration fee varies based on the participant type:
Students: ₹1,500
Professionals: ₹3,000

5. Can I present a paper at the conference?

Absolutely! We welcome paper presentations. Please submit your abstracts via the website. The deadline for submissions is 31st December 2023.

6. Will accommodation be provided?

Yes, accommodation will be arranged for outstation participants. Details and booking options will be provided during registration.

7. Who can I contact for further queries?

You can reach out to our help desk at conference@nitjsr.ac.in or call +91-9876543210.
